The Story of HenAnn POS

In the heart of a bustling Kenyan marketplace, where small businesses are the lifeblood of the economy, a challenge was quietly brewing. Shopkeepers were juggling handwritten receipts, manual stocktaking, and endless end-of-day calculations. Mistakes were common, sales were lost, and stock often ran out unexpectedly. It was time for a change. Enter HenAnn POS — a lightweight, modern point-of-sale system, built with Laravel Jetstream, tailored to the real needs of local business owners. It wasn't just another POS — it was built from the ground up with simplicity, speed, and reliability in mind. Designed by a developer who understood the rhythm of small shops and wholesale stores, HenAnn POS runs smoothly as a desktop application via Electron, needing no complicated installations or internet connections. Just open it, and it's ready to go. One central machine acts as the server, while multiple others connect on the local network — no fancy infrastructure required. Behind the scenes, Laravel powers the logic, while jobs like automated stock deduction are queued in the background for maximum speed and accuracy. With Supervisor managing the queue workers silently, HenAnn ensures that every sale reflects immediately in your inventory — no delay, no error. From barcode scanning to customer tracking, from wholesale pricing to flexible units of measure, HenAnn POS doesn’t just sell products — it empowers businesses to grow, scale, and thrive in the digital age. Whether you're selling sugar in sacks, groceries in grams, or electronics by the unit, HenAnn POS is your silent, reliable partner at the counter.
